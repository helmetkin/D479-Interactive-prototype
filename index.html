WELCOME TO TANITI
Discover the Beauty and Adventure of Taniti Island
Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific with less than 500 square miles of diverse terrain‚Äîfeaturing sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior with a small, active volcano. Home to about 20,000 indigenous residents, Taniti offers visitors a unique blend of natural wonders, vibrant culture, and a growing tourism scene. Once dominated by fishing and agriculture, the island now welcomes travelers from around the world to experience its charm and hospitality.
Explore
Enjoy beaches, rainforest hikes, and volcano adventures. Taniti City and Merriton Landing offer museums, art galleries, pubs, and a new dance club.
Stay
Choose from a variety of lodging options: family-owned hotels, bed & breakfasts, a hostel, or a four-star resort. All accommodations are regulated for quality and safety.
Dine
Savor local fish and rice, American-style meals, and Pan-Asian cuisine at Taniti‚Äôs 10 restaurants. Stock up at supermarkets, grocery stores, or the 24-hour convenience store.
Getting Around: Arrive by air or cruise ship. Public and private buses, taxis, rental cars, and bikes make exploring easy. Taniti City is flat and walkable.
Visitor Info: U.S. dollar is the currency; power outlets are 120V. English is widely spoken by younger residents. The island is safe, but watch for petty crime as tourism grows.
