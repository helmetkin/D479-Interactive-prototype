BOOKING
Find your perfect place to stay and plan your activities in Taniti.
Lodging: Choose from a hostel, family-owned hotels, bed and breakfasts, or a four-star resort. All lodging is regulated and inspected for quality.
Dining: Enjoy 10 restaurants (local, American, and Pan-Asian), plus several grocery stores and a 24-hour convenience store.
Activities: Book snorkeling, fishing tours, rainforest hikes, volcano visits, zip-lining, and more.
Transportation: Arrange for taxis, rental cars, bikes, or use public/private buses to get around the island. Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m to 11 p.m every day.
